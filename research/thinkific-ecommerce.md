# Thinkific E-Commerce Research Notes

*Research Date: March 24, 2026*
*Sources: Thinkific Support Documentation, Thinkific Blog*

## Core E-Commerce Feature: TCommerce

### What is TCommerce?
TCommerce ("Thinkific Commerce") is Thinkific's built-in payment processing system powered by Stripe Connect. It's the foundation for all advanced e-commerce features.

### Key Capabilities

#### 1. Payment Processing
- **Thinkific Payments** processes payments in 130+ currencies via Stripe Connect
- Supports Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Buy Now Pay Later (Klarna/Afterpay)
- No platform transaction fees on paid plans — only standard Stripe rates (2.9% + 30¢)

#### 2. Order Bumps
- Offer additional products during checkout with a single checkbox
- Can be applied to any published product (courses, communities, bundles)
- Order bumps have their own price (can be discounted)
- Supports strikethrough pricing to show discount value
- Cannot be combined with Gifting or Group Orders
- Only one order bump per primary product
- Tracking available in Revenue Dashboard with "Order Bump" column

#### 3. One-Click Upsells (After Purchase Flow)
- Post-purchase upsells immediately after checkout
- Custom Thank You pages
- Redirect to specific products or custom URLs

#### 4. Automated Tax Remittance (Major Differentiator)
**US & Canada:**
- Automatic calculation, collection, AND remittance of sales tax
- Thinkific pays taxes to authorities on your behalf
- Enabled by default for all TCommerce accounts
- Tax-inclusive pricing option available

**UK & EU (VAT):**
- Automatic calculation and collection of VAT
- Thinkific pays VAT to you as part of payouts
- YOU are responsible for remitting VAT under your own company tax return
- Must be manually enabled by Site Owner

#### 5. Affiliate Programs
- Available on Growth+ plans
- Commission calculated only on primary product (not order bumps)
- Revenue Partners get percentage of product sales regardless of whether offered as primary or order bump

#### 6. Group Orders
- Allow organizations to purchase multiple seats at once
- Cannot be combined with Order Bumps

#### 7. Gifting
- Students can purchase courses as gifts
- Cannot be combined with Order Bumps

### Limitations
- Order bumps not available with local currency at checkout
- Subscription/payment plan products have PayPal limitations
- Pre-order products cannot be used as order bumps
- TCommerce only available in certain countries (US, Canada, UK, EU primarily)

### Plan Availability
- TCommerce available on: Pro + Growth, Premier, Grow, Expand, Plus
- Order Bumps: Available on all paid plans with TCommerce
- Sales Tax Solution: All TCommerce accounts

### Competitive Position
- **Unique advantage:** One of only two platforms (with Teachable:pay) offering automated tax remittance
- **Revenue impact:** Thinkific reports TCommerce users sell up to 31% more than basic Stripe users
- **Transaction fees:** 0% on paid plans (vs Teachable's 5-10% on lower plans, Podia's 8% on free plan)
