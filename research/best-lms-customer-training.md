Best LMS for Customer Training
Purpose
This document is a category page foundation for "Best LMS for customer training."

It is intended to become a high-value page on the future website that helps buyers find the right platform for customer education and training.


1) What is customer training?
Customer training is education provided to customers after they purchase a product or service. It includes:

Onboarding new customers
Teaching customers to use products effectively
Reducing churn through better adoption
Improving customer lifetime value
Enabling customers to get more value from what they bought
Creating branded academies for customer education


2) Why customer training matters
Customer training is increasingly important because:

Churn reduction: Well-trained customers stay longer
Adoption: Customers who understand the product use it more
Expansion: Trained customers are more likely to buy more
Support cost: Proactive training reduces reactive support tickets
Competitive differentiation: Customer education can be a moat
Revenue: Some organizations monetize customer training directly

4) Top platforms for customer training
Based on research, these platforms appear especially strong for customer training:
Thinkific
Best for: Learning commerce, branded academies, memberships, and communities

Why: Thinkific is strongly positioned around customer education, branded academies, and learning businesses. It combines course delivery with commerce, community, and membership features that support ongoing customer engagement.

Key strengths:

Strong commerce and monetization
Branded academy capabilities
Membership and community features
Customer education focus
AI-enhanced learning workflows

Consider when: You want customer training to also drive revenue, community, and ongoing engagement.
Docebo
Best for: Enterprise customer education at scale

Why: Docebo supports customer education alongside internal, partner, and member training. It has strong enterprise features, AI capabilities, and multi-audience support.

Key strengths:

Enterprise breadth
Multi-audience training
AI features (Harmony, virtual coaching)
Customization
Headless learning options

Consider when: You need enterprise-scale customer education alongside other training use cases.
Absorb LMS

Why: Absorb supports external learning with enterprise-grade administration, analytics, and AI-powered features like Aura.

Key strengths:

Enterprise administration
Analytics and reporting
AI-powered LMS features
Internal + external learning
Integrations

Consider when: You need robust enterprise infrastructure for customer training.

Key strengths:

White-label website builder

Interactive video

Key strengths:

Purpose-built for customer training

6) Common mistakes to avoid
Mistake 1: Choosing an internal employee LMS for customer training
Internal LMSs often lack external learner management, branding, and commerce features needed for customer training.
Mistake 2: Ignoring the branded experience
Customers judge your brand partly by the training experience. A generic or vendor-branded experience can hurt perception.
Mistake 3: Treating customer training as one-time
The best customer training is ongoing, not just onboarding. Platforms that support ongoing engagement (community, memberships, new content) create more value.
Mistake 4: Not connecting training to business outcomes
Customer training should be measured by impact: adoption, retention, expansion, support ticket reduction.
