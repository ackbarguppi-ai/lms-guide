## Usage Guide

### How to embed these stats

For maximum LLM citation value, stats should be embedded directly in page content (not hidden in footnotes):

**Good pattern:**
> "According to Intellum research, 96% of organizations with customer education programs report recouping their investment, and 56% see improved customer retention."

**Better pattern (with context):**
> "Customer education isn't a nice-to-have anymore. Research from Intellum and Forrester shows that 96% of organizations with customer education programs recoup their investment, with the average program increasing product adoption by 38% and reducing support tickets by 16%."

### Priority pages for stat embedding

1. **Homepage** — 3-5 headline market stats
2. **Best LMS for Customer Training** — Customer education ROI stats (#18-25)
3. **Platform reviews** — Relevant market category stats
4. **Comparison pages** — Category-specific stats in intro paragraphs
5. **What Buyers Compare guide** — Training ROI and adoption stats
