## Best Sources to Deep-Dive

### Buyer-Specific (Customer Education / External Training)

1. **2025 Customer LMS Buyers Guide — Talented Learning**
   https://talentedlearning.com/learning-systems-research/customer-lms-buyers-guide/
   Dedicated buyer's guide for customer-facing LMS; benchmarks platforms against customer education requirements.

2. **How to Choose the Right LMS for Customer Training — TechClass**
   https://www.techclass.com/resources/learning-and-development-articles/how-to-choose-the-right-lms-for-customer-training
   Focused specifically on customer training LMS selection criteria and decision factors.

3. **Looking For a Customer Education Platform? 10 Things to Know — Check-n-Click**
   https://check-n-click.com/looking-for-a-customer-education-platform-here-are-10-things-you-should-know-before-you-buy/

4. **How to Choose an LMS for Customer Education — Northpass**
   https://www.northpass.com/blog/how-to-choose-an-lms-for-customer-education

5. **LMS for Customer Education and Training — Docebo**
   https://www.docebo.com/learning-network/blog/customer-education-lms/
   Guide to choosing LMS for customer education with key deciding factors including customizability and scalability.

6. **How to Start a Training Academy Platform in 2026 — Blend-ed**
   https://www.blend-ed.com/blog/how-to-launch-a-training-academy-platform

### General LMS Evaluation Frameworks & Checklists

7. **LMS Evaluation Criteria and Checklist for 2025 — Docebo**
   https://www.docebo.com/learning-network/blog/lms-criteria/
   Comprehensive evaluation checklist template with rubric-style criteria across multiple LMS dimensions.

8. **LMS Evaluation Guide and Checklist for 2026 — iSpring**
   https://www.ispring.com/knowledge-hub/lms-evaluation

9. **The Complete LMS Evaluation Guide — D2L**
   https://www.d2l.com/blog/the-complete-lms-evaluation-guide/

10. **Criteria For Evaluating Your LMS — eLearning Industry**
    https://elearningindustry.com/evaluating-your-lms-criteria

11. **Deconstructing LMS RFPs: The Most Popular Evaluation Criteria — ListedTech**
    https://listedtech.com/blog/deconstructing-lms-rfps-the-most-popular-evaluation-criteria/

12. **Five Steps to Evaluate and Select an LMS — Learning Guild**
    https://www.learningguild.com/articles/five-steps-to-evaluate-and-select-an-lms-proven-practices
