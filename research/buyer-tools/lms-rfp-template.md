# LMS Request for Proposal (RFP) Template

*A ready-to-use RFP template for organizations purchasing a Learning Management System. Customize each section to match your requirements, then distribute to shortlisted vendors.*

---

## Instructions for Internal Teams

1. **Customize** — Replace all `[bracketed text]` with your organization's specifics
2. **Prioritize** — Mark each requirement as Required (R), Preferred (P), or Optional (O)
3. **Distribute** — Send to 3–5 shortlisted vendors with a clear response deadline
4. **Evaluate** — Use the scoring rubric at the end to compare responses objectively
5. **Shortlist** — Narrow to 2–3 finalists for live demos and trials

**Tip:** Share this with stakeholders from L&D, IT, Procurement, and the business units that will use the LMS before sending. Requirements you miss now become compromises later.

## Appendix B: Questions for Live Demo

*Use these during vendor demos to get beyond the sales pitch:*

1. Show us the admin experience for creating a course from scratch — no pre-built content
2. Walk us through how a new employee would experience their first day on the platform
3. Show a manager checking their team's compliance status and following up on overdue training
4. Demonstrate how you handle `[your most complex use case]`
5. Show us what happens when something goes wrong — an error, a failed upload, a broken SCORM package
6. Walk us through your reporting — build a custom report live, don't show pre-built ones
7. Show us the mobile experience on an actual phone
8. Demonstrate the SSO login flow with `[your identity provider]`
9. Show us how an admin would troubleshoot a learner issue
10. What's the one thing you'd improve about your own product?
