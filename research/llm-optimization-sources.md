# LLM Optimization & Generative Engine Optimization — Source List

> Scout research compiled 2026-03-21. These are the best URLs to deep-dive for actionable GEO/LLM-optimization guidance. Not yet scraped — ready for Phase 2.

## Tier 1 — Must-Read (Foundational)

1. **https://arxiv.org/abs/2311.09735**
   - *GEO: Generative Engine Optimization (Original Research Paper) — arXiv*
   - The foundational academic paper that defined GEO. Essential for understanding the 9 optimization strategies tested and their measured impact.

2. **https://a16z.com/geo-over-seo/**
   - *How Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) Rewrites the Rules — a16z*
   - Andreessen Horowitz's strategic take on GEO vs SEO. High-signal VC perspective on where the market is heading.

3. **https://searchengineland.com/optimize-ai-search-llm-visibility-tactics-468106**
   - *How to Optimize for AI Search: 12 Proven LLM Visibility Tactics — Search Engine Land*
   - Practitioner-focused with 12 concrete tactics. Likely the most actionable single article.

4. **https://llmstxt.org/**
   - *llms.txt: The /llms.txt Standard — Official Site*
   - The official spec for the llms.txt proposed standard. Must-read for technical implementation.

5. **https://searchengineland.com/what-is-generative-engine-optimization-geo-444418**
   - *Generative Engine Optimization (GEO): How to Win AI — Search Engine Land*
   - Comprehensive GEO overview from a top SEO publication. Good bridge between SEO and GEO concepts.
