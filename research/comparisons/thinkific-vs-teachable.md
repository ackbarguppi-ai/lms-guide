# Thinkific vs Teachable (2026): An Honest Comparison

Thinkific and Teachable are the two most-compared online course platforms — and for good reason. Both let you build, sell, and deliver courses without writing code. But they've diverged significantly in pricing structure, feature priorities, and who they serve best.

**The short version:** Thinkific gives you unlimited courses on every plan with stronger community and compliance features, but charges a surcharge (1–5%) if you use your own Stripe account. Teachable caps published products but includes a native mobile app for students on all plans and handles tax compliance automatically. Neither has a free plan anymore.

This guide breaks down exactly where each platform wins, where they fall short, and which one fits your business — whether you're a solo expert selling your first course, an academy scaling a training catalog, or a company adding customer education.

## Pricing Breakdown

Pricing is where these platforms differ most — not just in sticker price, but in how fees add up.

### Thinkific Pricing (2026)

| Plan | Monthly | Annual | Transaction Fee | Key Limits |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Basic** | $49/mo | $36/mo | 0% (Thinkific Payments) / 5% (own Stripe) | 10,000 students, 5 digital downloads, 1 community |
| **Start** | $99/mo | $74/mo | 0% / 2% (own Stripe) | 10,000 students, unlimited downloads |
| **Grow** | $199/mo | $149/mo | 0% / 1% (own Stripe) | 10,000 students, 3 communities |
| **Plus** | Custom | Custom | Custom | Unlimited students, SCORM, SSO |

**Key pricing detail:** Thinkific charges 0% transaction fees *only* if you use Thinkific Payments (their built-in processor at ~2.9% standard processing). If you connect your own Stripe account, Thinkific adds a surcharge on top of Stripe's standard fees: 5% on Basic, 2% on Start, and 1% on Grow. On the Basic plan, that means total processing costs of ~7.9% per transaction with your own Stripe.

### Teachable Pricing (2026)

| Plan | Monthly | Annual | Transaction Fee | Key Limits |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Starter** | $39/mo | $29/mo | 7.5% | 5 published products, 100 students, 1 admin |
| **Builder** | $89/mo | $69/mo | 0% | 10 products, 1,000 students, 1 admin |
| **Growth** | $189/mo | $139/mo | 0% | 50 products, 5,000 students, 5 admins |
| **Advanced** | $399/mo | $309/mo | 0% | 100 products, 5,000 students, 5 admins |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Custom | 0% | Custom limits, SSO, dedicated manager |

**Key pricing detail:** Teachable's Starter plan has a 7.5% transaction fee on every sale, which can be steep. However, from the Builder plan ($69/mo annual) onward, transaction fees drop to 0%. The tradeoff is Teachable caps the number of published products — 5 on Starter, 10 on Builder, 50 on Growth, 100 on Advanced. Unpublished drafts and memberships/bundles don't count toward the cap.

### Real-World Cost Comparison

To make this concrete, here's what each platform costs at $5,000/month in course revenue:

The takeaway: if you want to use your own Stripe account, Teachable is significantly cheaper at the mid-tier. If you're fine using the platform's built-in payment processor, the difference narrows considerably.

## Course Building & Content

### Thinkific

Thinkific's course builder is drag-and-drop with support for video, text, quizzes, surveys, presentations, multimedia, and downloadable resources. Courses can be structured with chapters and lessons. On the Start plan and above, you get assignments, completion certificates, and course compliance tools (useful for professional development or continuing education).

SCORM compliance is available on the Plus plan, making Thinkific one of the few self-serve platforms that supports importing interactive e-learning content built in tools like Articulate or Adobe Captivate.

Thinkific also supports scheduled/drip content on all plans, letting you release lessons over time rather than all at once.

### Teachable

Teachable's course builder supports video, text, quizzes (auto-graded and short answer), and file uploads. It includes AI-powered course creation tools on all plans, plus free subtitles and automatic translations — a notable differentiator for experts with international audiences.

Certificates are available on all Teachable plans, whereas Thinkific requires the Start plan ($74/mo annual) or above.

Teachable natively supports three product types beyond courses: coaching, digital downloads, and memberships. This makes it more versatile for experts who sell a mix of products — for example, a $29 ebook, a $500 course, and a $200/mo coaching package, all from one storefront.

### Verdict

**Thinkific wins on:** advanced course features (SCORM, compliance tools, exams), unlimited course publishing on every plan, and depth of the course builder.

**Teachable wins on:** product versatility (native coaching, digital downloads), AI creation tools, automatic subtitles/translations, and certificates on every plan.

## Community Features

### Thinkific

Thinkific has a purpose-built community product with spaces, threaded replies, user profiles, mentions, DMs, and live events. The Basic plan includes 1 community with 5 spaces; the Grow plan includes 3 communities with 20 spaces each. Communities can be bundled with courses or sold as standalone memberships.

The community feature is genuinely integrated into the learning experience — not bolted on as an afterthought.

### Teachable

Teachable launched its community feature more recently. It supports discussion threads, student interaction, and integration with courses. While functional, it's less mature than Thinkific's offering in terms of spaces, DMs, and event hosting.

### Verdict

**Thinkific wins on community.** More spaces, DMs, live events within communities, and deeper integration. If community is central to your business model, Thinkific is the stronger choice.

## Payment Processing & Fees

This is where the decision gets financially significant.

### Thinkific

Thinkific Payments (powered by their own processor) charges 0% platform transaction fees with standard ~2.9% processing. If you use your own Stripe account, Thinkific adds a surcharge: **5% on Basic, 2% on Start, 1% on Grow**. PayPal is only available on the Grow plan ($149/mo annual) and above.

Thinkific Payments gives you access to TCommerce features: order bumps, Buy Now Pay Later, gifting, group orders, smart credit card retries, and incomplete purchase reminders.

### Teachable

teachable:pay (their built-in processor) handles payments with automated U.S. sales tax, global VAT, and GST compliance. The Starter plan carries a **7.5% transaction fee** on every sale. From Builder ($69/mo annual) onward, transaction fees are 0%.

Teachable's automatic tax handling is a significant operational advantage — you don't need third-party tax software or manual compliance for international sales.

### Verdict

**Teachable wins on tax automation** — built-in VAT/GST handling is a real time-saver, especially for experts selling internationally.

**Thinkific wins on flexibility** if you're on the Start or Grow plan and use Thinkific Payments (0% fees, more commerce tools). But the Stripe surcharge is a genuine penalty if you need your own payment processor for accounting or business reasons.

**Critical note:** Teachable's Starter plan at 7.5% and Thinkific's Basic plan at 5% (own Stripe) are both expensive for anyone doing meaningful revenue. Budget for at least the mid-tier plan on either platform.

## Mobile Experience

### Thinkific

Thinkific does not include a mobile app on standard plans. A **branded mobile app** (your own iOS and Android app) is available as an add-on for **$199/month** — a significant expense on top of your plan fee. Without the add-on, students access courses through a mobile-responsive website.

### Teachable

Teachable includes **iOS and Android student apps on all plans** at no extra cost. Students can download the Teachable app, log in, and access their purchased courses with offline viewing. The app is Teachable-branded (not white-labeled), but it's functional and free.

### Verdict

**Teachable wins decisively on mobile.** A native app on every plan vs. a $199/mo add-on is a major difference, especially since mobile learning consumption continues to grow. If your audience expects a mobile app experience, Teachable delivers it at no extra cost.

## Who Should Choose Thinkific

### Solo Experts

Choose Thinkific if you plan to sell **many courses** and want unlimited publishing from day one. The Start plan ($74/mo annual) is the practical starting point — it unlocks certificates, assignments, Zoom, payment plans, and memberships. The Basic plan is too limited for most.

**Best plan:** Start ($74/mo annual)

### Academies & Training Businesses

Thinkific's community features, group orders, invoicing, and compliance tools make it well-suited for training businesses managing multiple programs. The 10,000 student cap is the main constraint — if you're approaching that limit, you'll need the custom-priced Plus plan.

**Best plan:** Grow ($149/mo annual) or Plus

### Companies Adding Customer Education

Thinkific Plus is the clear choice for companies that need SCORM compliance, SSO, learning paths, CRM integrations, and dedicated support. No standard Teachable plan matches this feature set for corporate/enterprise training.

**Best plan:** Plus (custom pricing)

---

## Who Should Choose Teachable

### Solo Experts

Choose Teachable if you sell a **mix of product types** — courses, coaching, digital downloads — and want a native mobile app included. The Builder plan ($69/mo annual) is the sweet spot: 0% transaction fees, 10 products, and all core features.

**Best plan:** Builder ($69/mo annual)

### Academies & Training Businesses

Teachable works for academies that need to scale up to 50 products and 5,000 students. The Growth plan ($139/mo annual) offers custom reporting, branding removal, and 5 admin seats. The product cap (50) is the main constraint — if you have a large catalog, you'll need Advanced or Enterprise.

**Best plan:** Growth ($139/mo annual)

### Companies Adding Customer Education

Teachable's Enterprise plan includes SSO, dedicated account management, and migration assistance, but lacks SCORM support. If your company doesn't need SCORM and prioritizes ease of use and tax automation, Teachable Enterprise is viable. For compliance-heavy industries, look elsewhere.

**Best plan:** Enterprise (custom pricing)

## Final Verdict

**Choose Thinkific if:**
- You need unlimited courses and plan to build a large catalog
- Community engagement is core to your model
- You require SCORM compliance or corporate training features
- You're comfortable using Thinkific Payments to avoid surcharges
- You sell primarily to other businesses (B2B features like group orders and invoicing)

**Choose Teachable if:**
- You want a native mobile app without paying extra
- You sell multiple product types (courses + coaching + downloads)
- International tax compliance matters and you don't want to manage it yourself
- You need certificates and affiliates on your entry plan
- You prefer a lower starting price with 0% transaction fees from the mid-tier

Both platforms are mature, reliable, and actively developed. The "wrong" choice isn't catastrophic — migration is possible, if inconvenient. Pick the one that matches your business model today and your growth trajectory for the next 12–18 months.
