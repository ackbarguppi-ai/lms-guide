# Thinkific vs LearnWorlds: Which LMS Platform Should You Choose in 2026?

Thinkific and LearnWorlds are two of the most capable online course platforms available, but they serve different strengths. **Thinkific is the stronger all-in-one platform** for experts selling courses alongside memberships, coaching, and digital downloads. **LearnWorlds wins on interactive course delivery** with built-in interactive video, more course player layouts, and SCORM support on lower-tier plans. Your best choice depends on whether you need breadth of product types or depth of learning features.

Here's the full breakdown.

## Pricing: What You'll Actually Pay

### Thinkific Plans (2026)

| Plan | Monthly | Annual (per month) | Transaction Fees | Key Limits |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Basic** | $49/mo | $36/mo | 0% (Thinkific Payments) or 5% (own Stripe) | 10,000 students; 5 digital downloads; no certificates/assignments |
| **Start** | $99/mo | $74/mo | 0% (Thinkific Payments) or 2% (own Stripe) | 10,000 students; unlocks memberships, certificates, Zoom |
| **Grow** | $199/mo | $149/mo | 0% (Thinkific Payments) or 1% (own Stripe) | 10,000 students; removes branding; API/webhooks |
| **Plus** | Custom | Custom | Custom | Unlimited students; SCORM; SSO; branded app on select plans |

**Key pricing detail:** Thinkific charges no transaction fees if you use their built-in payment processor (Thinkific Payments / TCommerce). If you connect your own Stripe account, there's a per-transaction surcharge of 1–5% depending on plan — on top of Stripe's standard 2.9% + $0.30. At $5,000/mo in revenue on the Basic plan with own Stripe, that surcharge alone costs $250/month.

### LearnWorlds Plans (2026)

| Plan | Monthly | Annual (per month) | Transaction Fees | Key Limits |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Starter** | $29/mo | $24/mo | $5 per course sale | 1 admin; 3-page website; basic quizzes only |
| **Pro Trainer** | $99/mo | $79/mo | None | 5 admins; unlimited pages; 20 SCORMs; certifications |
| **Learning Center** | $299/mo | $249/mo | None | 25 admins; white-label; interactive video; automations |
| **High Volume & Corporate** | Custom | Custom | None | Dedicated success manager; SLA; custom admin seats |

**Key pricing detail:** LearnWorlds' Starter plan looks cheap at $24–29/month, but the $5-per-sale fee adds up fast. Selling 20 courses per month means $100 in fees on top of your plan cost. The break-even point is roughly 14 sales/month — beyond that, Pro Trainer ($79–99/mo) with zero transaction fees is more economical.

### Price Reality Check

For an expert doing $3,000/month in course revenue:

- **Thinkific Start (annual):** $74/mo + $0 transaction fees (using Thinkific Payments) = **$74/mo total platform cost**
- **Thinkific Start (annual, own Stripe):** $74/mo + ~$60 surcharge (2% of $3K) = **$134/mo total**
- **LearnWorlds Pro Trainer (annual):** $79/mo + $0 transaction fees = **$79/mo total platform cost**
- **LearnWorlds Starter (annual):** $24/mo + ~$100 in per-sale fees (est. 20 sales) = **$124/mo total**

At this revenue level, both platforms' mid-tier plans land in a similar range ($74–79/month annually). The difference comes down to features, not price.

## Category-by-Category Breakdown

### 1. Course Creation & Delivery

**LearnWorlds wins this category.**

LearnWorlds was built course-first, and it shows. The platform offers five different course player layouts, a built-in interactive video editor (add in-video quizzes, clickable elements, branching paths), and AI-powered subtitle generation. You can build learning paths that sequence courses into structured programs with progression rules.

Thinkific's course builder is clean and intuitive — drag-and-drop curriculum building, drip scheduling, and AI-generated course outlines. It supports video, text, quizzes, and assignments. But it lacks native interactive video, and the course player is more standardized.

Both platforms support:
- AI-assisted course outline generation
- Quizzes and exams
- Drip/scheduled content release
- In-course discussions
- Multimedia lessons (video, audio, PDF, text)

**LearnWorlds advantages:**
- Interactive video with in-video quizzes and annotations
- 5 course player layouts vs. Thinkific's single layout
- Learning paths with structured progression
- Gradebook for managing student grades across assessments
- SCORM support starting at $99/mo (Pro Trainer — 20 SCORMs)

**Thinkific advantages:**
- Simpler, faster course setup for straightforward content
- Assignments and certificates available at $74/mo (Start)
- Completion certificates with custom branding

### 2. Product Versatility (Beyond Courses)

**Thinkific wins this category.**

Thinkific lets you sell courses, memberships, coaching sessions, live webinars, digital downloads (e-books, templates, checklists, guides), and product bundles — all as standalone products. This matters for experts who want to monetize beyond just courses.

LearnWorlds is course-centric. You can't sell standalone digital downloads — they can only be attached as resources within a course. Memberships and subscriptions exist but are tied to course enrollment rather than operating independently. If you're building a business around a portfolio of digital products, this is a meaningful limitation.

**What Thinkific sells natively:**
- Online courses (on-demand and live)
- Coaching sessions (1:1 and group via Zoom)
- Memberships and subscriptions
- Digital downloads (e-books, templates, PDFs, checklists)
- Product bundles combining any of the above

**What LearnWorlds sells natively:**
- Online courses (on-demand and live)
- Course-based subscriptions and programs
- 1:1 and group sessions
- Bundles of courses

### 3. Community & Engagement

**Slight edge to LearnWorlds on flexibility; Thinkific wins on integration.**

LearnWorlds offers unlimited communities on all plans, which is generous. Communities support discussions, events, and collaboration spaces for students.

Thinkific's community feature is more tightly integrated with the rest of the platform. You get 1 community on Basic/Start, 3 on Grow, and unlimited on Plus. Communities include spaces (sub-sections), threaded replies, DMs, member profiles, weekly digests, and live events. The community can be bundled with courses and sold as part of a membership — creating a cohesive product experience.

For training businesses running multiple cohorts or programs, Thinkific's ability to segment communities into spaces and tie them to specific products is valuable.

### 4. Website & Design

**LearnWorlds has a slight design edge; Thinkific is more practical.**

LearnWorlds offers more website templates and greater visual customization options. The site builder supports extensive widget-based layouts, and the Learning Center plan includes white-labeling (remove LearnWorlds branding).

Thinkific provides a solid site builder on all plans with landing pages, custom domains, and CSS/HTML editing on Start and above. Removing Thinkific branding requires the Grow plan ($149/mo).

One important caveat: LearnWorlds' Starter plan limits you to a 3-page website. For a full site, you need Pro Trainer ($99/mo) or higher.

### 5. Marketing & Sales Tools

**Thinkific wins on sales tools; LearnWorlds wins on forms and qualification.**

Thinkific's marketing toolkit includes:
- Email automation (built-in)
- Sales funnels with upsells and order bumps
- Abandoned cart recovery (all plans)
- Affiliate reporting
- Coupons and promotions
- Post-purchase upsell flows
- Landing page builder

LearnWorlds offers:
- Affiliate management (Pro Trainer+)
- Marketing forms and qualification forms
- NPS surveys
- Page funnels (15 on Pro Trainer, unlimited on Learning Center)
- Mass email (Pro Trainer+)
- Pop-ups (Pro Trainer+)

Thinkific's abandoned cart emails are available on all plans — a feature that directly recovers revenue. LearnWorlds' qualification forms and NPS surveys are unique and useful for academies that need to segment or qualify incoming students.

### 6. SCORM & Compliance

**LearnWorlds wins for most buyers; Thinkific wins at enterprise scale.**

SCORM compliance is critical for companies delivering formal training or professional development that needs to integrate with existing LMS infrastructure.

- **LearnWorlds:** SCORM available starting at Pro Trainer ($99/mo) with 20 SCORM packages; unlimited on Learning Center ($299/mo)
- **Thinkific:** SCORM only on Plus (custom pricing, typically $400+/mo)

For companies that need SCORM without enterprise pricing, LearnWorlds is significantly more accessible. However, Thinkific's Plus plan bundles SCORM with SSO, advanced security, Salesforce/HubSpot CRM integrations, and a dedicated customer success team — making it the stronger choice for large organizations.

### 7. Integrations & Ecosystem

**Thinkific has a broader ecosystem.**

Thinkific offers 96 integrations (per Stackreaction data) including a native app store, while LearnWorlds offers around 50+ native integrations. Both connect with Zapier for extended automation.

**Thinkific notable integrations:** HubSpot, Salesforce (Plus), QuickBooks, Zoom, Zapier, Stripe, PayPal, Mailchimp, ConvertKit, Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel

**LearnWorlds notable integrations:** HubSpot, Zoom, Webex, Microsoft Teams, Google Meet, Calendly, Zapier, Stripe, PayPal, Shopify, Mailchimp

LearnWorlds' advantage: native integrations with Webex, Microsoft Teams, and Calendly for live sessions — useful for academies and companies running regular scheduled training.

### 8. Payment Processing

**LearnWorlds offers more payment methods; Thinkific has a simpler model (with caveats).**

LearnWorlds supports Stripe, PayPal, Shopify, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Klarna, Afterpay, and regional European methods (iDEAL, Bancontact, Giropay, etc.) — processing payments in 23 currencies.

Thinkific supports Thinkific Payments (built-in), Stripe, and PayPal. PayPal requires the Grow plan ($149/mo). The surcharge structure for using your own Stripe (1–5%) is a genuine cost consideration that can add hundreds per month at scale.

For global businesses or those wanting buy-now-pay-later options, LearnWorlds offers significantly more flexibility.

### 9. Analytics & Reporting

**Thinkific wins on reporting depth.**

Thinkific provides dedicated dashboards for marketing, orders, revenue, enrollments, engagement, and custom reports. The engagement analytics show enrollment status, course completion rates, and lesson-level engagement data.

LearnWorlds offers a Reports Center with user progress, activity, engagement, and learning performance reports. Advanced reporting with scheduled reports and report templates is available on the Learning Center plan ($299/mo).

Both platforms provide the essentials, but Thinkific's analytics are more visual, more granular, and available on lower-tier plans.

### 10. AI Features

**LearnWorlds has more AI features across more plans.**

Both platforms have invested in AI, but LearnWorlds has pushed further:

| AI Feature | Thinkific | LearnWorlds |
|---|---|---|
| Course outline generation | All plans | All plans |
| AI teaching assistant | Plus only | — |
| AI translations | — | Pro Trainer+ |
| AI subtitle generation | — | Learning Center+ |
| AI-powered interactive video | — | Learning Center+ |
| AI content suggestions | Basic setup only | Course building + marketing |

Thinkific's AI teaching assistant (Plus only) is notable for enterprise use — it provides students with AI-powered help within courses. But for most experts and academies, LearnWorlds' AI tools are more accessible across plans.

## Who Should Choose What

### Choose Thinkific If You Are:

**An expert building a multi-product business.** You sell online courses but also want to offer coaching, digital downloads, memberships, and product bundles — all from one platform. Thinkific handles this natively; LearnWorlds doesn't.

**A company that wants simplicity with scale potential.** Thinkific's interface is faster to learn, the Plus plan offers enterprise features (SSO, CRM integrations, dedicated support), and the platform serves 35,000+ businesses across 100+ countries. If you're adding customer education to an existing business and want minimal friction, Thinkific is the safer bet.

**An expert who wants zero transaction fees without conditions.** Using Thinkific Payments means truly 0% platform fees on any plan. No per-sale charges, no surcharges.

### Choose LearnWorlds If You Are:

**An academy focused on course quality and interactive learning.** LearnWorlds' interactive video, multiple course player layouts, gradebook, and learning paths give you more control over the student experience. If your differentiation is course quality, LearnWorlds gives you better tools.

**A company that needs SCORM without enterprise pricing.** At $99/month (Pro Trainer), you get 20 SCORM packages — far more accessible than Thinkific's SCORM, which requires the custom-priced Plus plan.

**A training business serving global or European markets.** LearnWorlds' payment gateway diversity (23 currencies, Klarna, regional EU methods) and multi-language site support via Weglot make it better suited for international operations.

**An academy that wants white-label capabilities.** LearnWorlds' Learning Center plan ($299/mo) includes white-labeling, school cloning, and bulk licensing — features designed for organizations running training at scale under their own brand.

## The Verdict

**Thinkific is the better all-around platform** for most experts and companies. It handles more product types, has a simpler pricing model (when using Thinkific Payments), offers deeper analytics on lower tiers, and has a proven track record at scale. If you're building a business — not just a course — Thinkific gives you more room to grow.

**LearnWorlds is the better course delivery platform** for academies and training businesses where the learning experience is the product. Interactive video, SCORM at $99/month, learning paths, and five course layouts make it the stronger choice when course quality is your competitive advantage.

**The decision matrix:**

| If your priority is... | Choose |
|---|---|
| Selling multiple product types (courses + downloads + coaching) | **Thinkific** |
| Interactive, media-rich course experiences | **LearnWorlds** |
| Simplest path to launch and sell | **Thinkific** |
| SCORM compliance under $200/month | **LearnWorlds** |
| Enterprise features (SSO, CRM, dedicated support) | **Thinkific Plus** |
| Global payments with BNPL and EU methods | **LearnWorlds** |
| Largest integration ecosystem | **Thinkific** |
| White-label training platform for your brand | **LearnWorlds** |
| Community-driven membership business | **Thinkific** |
| AI-powered course creation tools on mid-tier plans | **LearnWorlds** |

Both platforms offer 30-day free trials. The best way to decide is to build a sample course on each and see which workflow fits your business.
