## 1. What Teachable Is & How It Positions Itself

### Company Overview
Teachable is an online course platform founded in 2014 by Ankur Nagpal, headquartered in New York City. In March 2020, the company was acquired by **Hotmart** (a Brazilian creator economy powerhouse) for **$250 million**, becoming part of what the company now describes as the "#1 worldwide leader in the creator economy" (combined with Hotmart).

### Market Positioning
Teachable positions itself as a **"learning commerce platform"** built for "creators, experts, entrepreneurs, and businesses who are serious about education." Their messaging emphasizes:

- **Full control**: "You get full control — you can talk to your students, match their learning styles, and build real relationships instead of going through third parties"
- **Business infrastructure**: They frame themselves as the operational backbone that handles "payments, taxes, and enrollments" so creators can focus on content
- **Global scale**: Heavy emphasis on international tax handling and multi-currency support

### Key Stats
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Creators on platform | 150,000+ |
| Students served | 100M+ |
| Countries active | 180+ |
| Revenue earned by creators | $10B+ (combined with Hotmart) |

### Target Market Segment
Teachable targets **solo experts and small-to-medium digital education businesses** rather than large academic institutions or enterprise training departments. Their ideal customer profile leans toward:
- Independent course creators (language teachers, fitness instructors, business coaches)
- Small academies selling premium courses ($200+ price points)
- Creators with existing audiences looking to monetize expertise

### Notable Customers
- **XelPlus Academy** (Leila Gharani) — 188 countries, data skills training
- **FlightInsight** (Dan George) — 10,000+ student flight school
- **Voxyde** (Razvan Ciobanu) — VFX/animation training
- **Speak Norsk** — Language learning, 14,000+ students
- **The Science of Reading** (Anna Geiger) — Educational content since 2017

## 2. Core Capabilities

### Product Types Supported
| Product | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Online Courses** | Video-based with quizzes, drip content, certificates |
| **Coaching** | 1:1 or group sessions with scheduling integration |
| **Digital Downloads** | Templates, guides, resources (PDFs, files) |
| **Memberships** | Subscription-based recurring revenue |
| **Community** | Built-in discussion forums (not counted against product limits) |

### Course Creation Features
- **Drag-and-drop course builder** with automatic saving
- **Video hosting** up to 4K resolution (up to 2TB storage on Advanced plan)
- **AI-powered tools**: Course outline generator, lesson draft generator, quiz generator, sales page writer
- **Content types**: Video, text, quizzes, code blocks, file attachments, embedded content
- **Drip content**: Schedule lesson releases by date or days-after-enrollment
- **Course compliance**: Track lesson completion, enforce video watching
- **Certificates**: Branded completion certificates (PDF generation)

### Assessment & Engagement
| Feature | Availability |
|---------|--------------|
| Auto-graded quizzes (multiple choice) | All paid plans |
| Short answer assessments | All paid plans |
| File upload assignments | Not available |
| Advanced quiz templates | Limited — described as "too basic" by some users |
| Gamification | Not available |

### Mobile Experience
- **Native iOS and Android apps** for students (included free on all plans)
- **Offline viewing** capability — students can download content
- **Screenshot prevention** and secure OTP login for content protection
- **Progress sync** across devices
- Key stat: Students using the mobile app are **2x more likely to return by day 3** and **3x more likely to complete lessons**

Note: The mobile app is Teachable-branded, not white-labeled. For a fully branded app, creators need to look elsewhere.

### Payments & Commerce (Teachable:Pay)
- **Payment methods**: Stripe, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay
- **Pricing models**: One-time, subscriptions, payment plans
- **Tax handling**: Automatic US sales tax, EU/UK VAT, and GST collection/remittance
- **Multi-currency**: 130+ currencies supported
- **Processing fees**: 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction (US cards)
- **Affiliate program**: Built-in affiliate management (Builder plan and above)
- **Sales tools**: Coupons, upsells, order bumps, abandoned cart emails, bundles

### Marketing Tools
- Basic built-in email marketing (broadcasts and simple automation)
- Landing page builder (limited customization)
- Student referral program
- Integration with: ConvertKit, Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, Zapier

### Analytics & Reporting
- Sales overviews and earnings by product
- Student progress tracking and completion rates
- Revenue breakdowns by date and pricing tier
- Basic reporting dashboard (advanced reporting only on higher tiers)

### Integrations

## 3. Pricing & Plans (Current as of March 2026)

### Major Pricing Restructure (June 2025)
Teachable eliminated their free plan in mid-2025 and restructured their entire pricing model. The changes were significant:
- **No more free plan** — replaced with a 7-day free trial
- **New plan names**: Starter, Builder, Growth, Advanced (replacing Free, Basic, Pro, Pro+, Business)
- **Product limits introduced**: Each plan caps the number of published products
- **Transaction fee increased** on entry plan: Now 7.5% on Starter (previously 10% on Free, 5% on Basic)

### Plan Comparison

| Plan | Monthly | Annual (per month) | Transaction Fee | Published Products | Admin Users | Students |
|------|---------|-------------------|-----------------|-------------------|-------------|----------|
| **Starter** | $39 | $29 | **7.5%** | 5 | 1 | 100 |
| **Builder** | $89 | $69 | 0% | 10 | 1 | 1,000 |
| **Growth** | $189 | $139 | 0% | 50 | 5 | Up to 5,000 |
| **Advanced** | $399 | $309 | 0% | 100 | 5 | Up to 5,000 |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Custom | 0% | Unlimited | Custom | Unlimited |

### What's Included by Plan

#### Starter ($29–$39/month)
- 5 published products
- 100 students
- 7.5% transaction fee
- Live chat support
- AI course creation tools
- iOS & Android student apps
- Global payments & tax handling
- Course certificates
- Unlimited integrations
- **Limitations**: No affiliate program, no custom domain on annual, limited admin tools

#### Builder ($69–$89/month)
- 10 published products
- 1,000 students
- **0% transaction fee**
- Built-in affiliate marketing
- Abandoned cart emails
- Course compliance tools
- Auto-graded quizzes
- Real-time support
- **Key upgrade**: Removes transaction fees, adds affiliate tools

#### Growth ($139–$189/month)
- 50 published products
- Up to 5,000 students
- Bulk student imports (2,000 included)
- Custom user roles
- Remove Teachable branding
- **Best for**: Growing businesses with multiple products

#### Advanced ($309–$399/month)
- 100 published products
- 2TB video storage
- Unlimited product drafts
- Priority support
- API: 25,000 requests/month
- **Best for**: Established creators with large catalogs

### Hidden Costs & Gotchas
1. **Payment processing fees**: 2.9% + $0.30 on ALL plans (in addition to any Teachable transaction fees)
2. **Product limits are strict**: Bundles, memberships, and communities don't count toward limits, but everything else does
3. **No free plan**: 7-day trial only, then you must pay
4. **Student import limits**: Starter allows 0 imported students; Growth includes 2,000
5. **Video storage caps**: 1TB on most plans, 2TB on Advanced
6. **API limits**: 10,000 requests/month on Builder, 25,000 on Advanced

### Annual Savings
All plans offer approximately **22% discount** for annual billing.

## 4. Strengths (What Teachable Does Best)

### 1. Mobile App Excellence
Teachable's native iOS and Android apps are genuinely best-in-class. Unlike many competitors that charge extra or don't offer apps at all, Teachable includes mobile access free on all plans. The offline viewing feature is particularly valuable for student engagement.

### 2. International Tax Handling
Automatic collection and remittance of US sales tax, EU VAT, and GST is a significant operational advantage. For creators selling globally, this eliminates a major administrative burden and compliance risk.

### 3. AI-Powered Content Tools
The AI hub (course outline generator, lesson drafts, quiz generator, subtitle/translation) provides genuine time savings. The automatic subtitle generation and translation into 70+ languages is particularly strong for global reach.

### 4. Ease of Use
Teachable consistently receives praise for being beginner-friendly. The course builder is intuitive, uploads are fast, and the interface is clean. Most creators can launch their first course within hours, not days.

### 5. Hotmart Backing
Being part of a major creator economy company provides stability, resources, and global payment infrastructure that smaller competitors can't match.

### 6. Transparent (if limiting) Product Structure
Unlike some competitors that gate features unpredictably, Teachable's product limits are clear upfront. You know exactly what you're paying for.

### 7. Reliable Infrastructure
Strong uptime, fast video delivery, and dependable payment processing. For creators who've experienced technical issues on other platforms, Teachable's stability is a selling point.

## 5. Weaknesses & Gaps (Honest Assessment)

### 1. Aggressive Pricing & Frequent Price Hikes
Teachable has raised prices multiple times:
- **2023**: Significant increases across all plans
- **June 2025**: Complete restructuring with higher entry prices and eliminated free plan
- **Builder plan jump**: From $39/month to $69/month (annual) — a **77% increase**

User sentiment from Trustpilot: *"10 Years of 'retired plans' and forced price hikes!"*

### 2. Punitive Transaction Fees on Entry Plan
The **7.5% transaction fee** on the Starter plan is steep. On a $100 course:
- Teachable fee: $7.50
- Payment processing: ~$3.20
- **Total fees: ~10.7%** — nearly $11 per sale

For comparison, Thinkific charges **0% transaction fees on all paid plans**.

### 3. Strict Product Limits
The 5-product cap on Starter and 10-product cap on Builder plans feel artificially limiting. As one reviewer noted: *"That 5-product cap can feel super limiting... if you're someone who likes to experiment with lower-ticket offers or tiered learning paths, you'll run out of slots sooner than you think."*

### 4. Limited Customization & Design
Teachable's site builder is functional but rigid. Key limitations:
- No site themes or templates
- Limited layout control
- No custom CSS/HTML on lower tiers
- All schools look similar

Review quote: *"Teachable's website-building capabilities are really limited at this point... they don't offer site themes, and the page builder has limited customization options."*

### 5. Basic Assessment Tools
The quiz builder is rudimentary:
- Only multiple choice and short answer
- No file upload assignments
- No advanced question types
- No robust assignment grading

For educators needing sophisticated assessments, this is a significant gap.

### 6. No Built-In Audience
Unlike marketplace platforms (Udemy, Skillshare), Teachable provides **zero organic traffic**. Every student must be acquired through the creator's own marketing efforts. This is by design but represents a major challenge for beginners without existing audiences.

### 7. Support Limitations
- Email-only on Starter plan
- 24-36 hour response times reported by users
- No phone support until higher tiers
- Some users report automated responses instead of human help

User complaint: *"I was asked to pay nearly $500/year but couldn't get a human to speak to before purchasing."*

### 8. Limited Community Features
While Teachable offers a community feature, it's described as a "quiet forum" — functional but not engaging like Circle, Discord, or dedicated community platforms. No gamification, limited organization tools.

### 9. No Free Tier
The elimination of the free plan in 2025 removes the ability for creators to test the platform or run a free course as a lead magnet without paying.

### 10. Student Limits on Lower Tiers
The 100-student cap on Starter and 1,000 on Builder can force premature upgrades for growing businesses.

## 6. Ideal Buyer Profile (Who Should Choose Teachable)

### Teachable Is BEST For:

**1. Solo Experts with Existing Audiences**
- You already have an email list, YouTube channel, or social following
- You want to monetize expertise with minimal technical complexity
- You value ease of use over deep customization

**2. Premium Course Sellers ($200+ price points)**
- Your courses command higher prices where transaction fees matter less
- You need reliable infrastructure for video delivery and payments
- International tax handling is important to your business

**3. Mobile-First Creators**
- Your students consume content primarily on mobile devices
- Offline access is valuable for your audience
- You don't want to pay extra for app development

**4. Global Sellers**
- You sell to students in multiple countries
- Automatic tax compliance is a priority
- Multi-currency support and translation features are valuable

**5. Hotmart Ecosystem Users**
- You're already using Hotmart products
- You want integrated creator economy tools
- You value the backing of a large, established company

### Teachable Is NOT For:

**1. Beginners Without Audiences**
- You don't have an existing following
- You're hoping the platform will bring students
- You need low-cost entry to test ideas

**2. Brand-Focused Businesses**
- Deep visual customization is critical
- You need unique, differentiated site design
- Your brand identity requires flexibility

**3. Assessment-Heavy Educators**
- You need sophisticated quizzes and assignments
- File uploads and detailed grading are required
- Academic rigor is a core value proposition

**4. High-Volume, Low-Price Sellers**
- You sell courses under $50
- Transaction fees would consume margins
- You need to scale to thousands of low-ticket sales

**5. Budget-Conscious Creators**
- You want to start free and scale gradually
- Predictable pricing without frequent hikes is important
- You don't want to worry about product limits

## 7. How It Compares to Thinkific

### Overview
Thinkific and Teachable are the two most direct competitors in the "learning commerce platform" space for independent creators. Both were founded around the same time (Thinkific 2012, Teachable 2014) and target similar audiences. However, their recent strategic directions have diverged.

### Pricing Comparison

| Factor | Teachable | Thinkific |
|--------|-----------|-----------|
| **Entry plan** | $29/mo (Starter, annual) | $36/mo (Basic, annual) |
| **Entry transaction fee** | **7.5%** | **0%** |
| **Mid-tier** | $69/mo (Builder, annual) | $74/mo (Start, annual) |
| **Growth tier** | $139/mo (Growth, annual) | $149/mo (Grow, annual) |
| **Free plan** | ❌ No (7-day trial) | ❌ No (30-day trial) |
| **Product limits** | Yes (5–100 depending on plan) | Unlimited courses on all paid plans |
| **Student limits** | Yes (100–5,000 depending on plan) | 10,000 on Basic–Grow, unlimited on Plus |

**Winner: Thinkific** — 0% transaction fees on all plans and unlimited courses provide better value for most creators.

### Feature Comparison

| Feature | Teachable | Thinkific |
|---------|-----------|-----------|
| **Native mobile apps** | ✅ Included free (Teachable-branded) | ❌ $199/mo add-on for branded app |
| **Course builder** | Simple drag-and-drop | Drag-and-drop with more flexibility |
| **Site customization** | Limited | More flexible with CSS/HTML access |
| **Quizzes** | Basic MCQ and short answer | More question types, assignments, exams |
| **Community** | Basic forum | More structured with Spaces |
| **Coaching** | ✅ Native | ✅ Native with Zoom integration |
| **Memberships** | ✅ Native | ✅ Native |
| **Digital downloads** | ✅ Native | ✅ Native (5 on Basic, unlimited above) |
| **SCORM compliance** | ❌ | ✅ Plus plan only |
| **AI tools** | Course outline, subtitles, quiz gen | Course outline generator, AI teaching assistant |
| **API access** | ✅ Limited by tier | ✅ Webhooks on Grow, full API on Plus |

**Winner: Depends on needs** — Teachable wins on mobile apps and AI subtitle generation; Thinkific wins on site flexibility, assessments, and SCORM.

### Commerce & Sales

| Feature | Teachable | Thinkific |
|---------|-----------|-----------|
| **Transaction fees** | 7.5% on Starter, 0% above | 0% on all paid plans |
| **Affiliate program** | Builder+ | Start+ |
| **Upsells/order bumps** | Builder+ | Basic+ |
| **Payment plans** | All paid | All paid |
| **Subscriptions** | All paid | All paid |
| **Abandoned cart** | Builder+ | Basic+ |
| **Tax handling** | Automatic US/EU/UK | Available via TCommerce |
| **Processing fees** | 2.9% + $0.30 | 2.9% (Thinkific Payments) |

**Winner: Thinkific** — 0% transaction fees on entry plan and earlier access to sales tools.

### Support & Service

| Factor | Teachable | Thinkific |
|--------|-----------|-----------|
| **Entry support** | Email only | Email and chat |
| **Response time** | 24-36 hours reported | Generally faster |
| **Live chat** | Builder+ | Basic+ |
| **Phone support** | Not on standard plans | Grow+ |
| **Dedicated success manager** | Enterprise only | Plus only |
| **User satisfaction** | 3.1/5 Trustpilot | Generally higher ratings |

**Winner: Thinkific** — Better support access on lower tiers.

### Key Differentiators

**Teachable Advantages:**
1. **Superior mobile apps** — Free, native iOS/Android apps included; Thinkific charges $199/mo for branded apps
2. **AI subtitle generation** — Automatic translation into 70+ languages is unique and valuable
3. **Hotmart ecosystem** — International payment infrastructure and creator economy backing
4. **Ease of use** — Generally considered more beginner-friendly

**Thinkific Advantages:**
1. **No transaction fees** — 0% on ALL paid plans vs. 7.5% on Teachable Starter
2. **Unlimited courses** — No artificial product limits on any paid plan
3. **More flexible site builder** — Better customization and design control
4. **Better assessments** — More quiz types, assignments, and exam features
5. **SCORM compliance** — Available for enterprise/corporate training needs
6. **More generous limits** — 10,000 students on Basic vs. 100 on Teachable Starter
7. **Longer trial** — 30 days vs. 7 days

### The Bottom Line

**Choose Teachable if:**
- Mobile learning experience is a priority
- You need AI-powered subtitle translation
- You want the simplest possible setup
- You're already in the Hotmart ecosystem
- You're selling premium courses where 7.5% fees matter less

**Choose Thinkific if:**
- You want the best value (0% fees, unlimited courses)
- Site customization and branding matter
- You need robust assessment tools
- You're building a scalable academy with many products
- You want better support on entry plans
