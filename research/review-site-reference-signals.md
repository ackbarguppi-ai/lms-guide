LMS Review-Site and Reference Signals
Purpose
This document captures early third-party review and reference-site signals for the LMS / learning commerce research project.

It is not the final review synthesis. It is the beginning of a source-backed reference layer that can later support:

website content
comparison pages
research citations
outbound reference links
fact-checking

Where possible, links are preserved for later reuse.

5) Implications for the future site
The future website should preserve and use source links because they support:

credibility
editorial discipline
easier updating
faster page building later
reference sections on comparison pages
internal research traceability

Recommended pattern for future research docs:

short conclusion
linked sources
extracted signal
interpretation
open questions

That will make the research corpus much more reusable during the build phase.


Bottom line
The research system should not just produce opinions. It should produce:

analysis
source links
reusable evidence trails

This document is the beginning of that layer.
