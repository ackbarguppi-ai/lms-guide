## Key Findings
- SCORM 1.2: universal except Kajabi, Teachable, Podia
- xAPI: enterprise platforms (Docebo, Absorb, TalentLMS, Moodle)
- SOC 2: Docebo, Absorb, Skilljar, TalentLMS standard; less common in expert platforms
- GDPR: requires DPA, data export/deletion, consent management
- Certificates: Thinkific custom templates + verification URLs, Docebo advanced management
- Digital credentials: Credly/Accredible integration via API/Zapier
- Recertification: enterprise platforms automate; expert platforms manual
