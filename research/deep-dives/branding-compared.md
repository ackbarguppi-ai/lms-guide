# LMS Branding & White-Label Options Compared

*Custom domains, branded apps, platform branding removal, and white-label reselling — how eight LMS platforms handle brand ownership, and which give you full control.*

---

Your brand is the reason learners trust you. When your LMS shows someone else's logo in the URL bar, the browser tab, or the mobile app store, it undermines that trust — sometimes subtly, sometimes not.

White-labeling and branding capabilities vary enormously across learning platforms. Some let you present a fully custom experience where learners never know which LMS powers it. Others keep their branding front and center, treating you more like a tenant than an owner.

This guide compares branding and white-label options across **Thinkific, Kajabi, LearnWorlds, Teachable, Podia, Docebo, Absorb LMS, and TalentLMS** — covering custom domains, branded mobile apps, platform branding removal, email customization, and white-label reselling.

---

## The Quick Verdict

**Most complete white-label for experts:** Thinkific and Kajabi both offer custom domains plus branded mobile apps, giving you full visual control over the learner experience on web and mobile.

**Best enterprise white-label:** Docebo leads with multi-tenant white-labeling, extended enterprise portals, and deep visual customization designed for organizations managing multiple brands or client audiences.

**Most limited branding:** Podia and TalentLMS offer basic customization but make full white-labeling difficult or impossible on lower plans.

## Custom Domains

A custom domain is the minimum viable branding requirement. Every serious platform supports this, but implementation quality varies.

### Thinkific

- **Custom domain** supported on all paid plans
- Full CNAME/DNS setup to point your domain (e.g., `learn.yourbrand.com` or `courses.yourbrand.com`) to your Thinkific site
- SSL certificate automatically provisioned
- No Thinkific branding in the URL
- Subdomain and root domain configurations supported
- **Site builder** allows extensive visual customization of the learning site, landing pages, and course pages

### Kajabi

- **Custom domain** on all paid plans
- Support for root domains and subdomains
- Automatic SSL provisioning
- Full DNS configuration through Kajabi's settings
- Multiple domains can be connected (useful for different product lines)
- Kajabi's website builder provides extensive page customization with templates and drag-and-drop editing

### Teachable

- **Custom domain** on all paid plans (including Basic)
- Subdomain and root domain support
- SSL included
- School-level branding with logo, colors, and typography customization
- Sales page builder with customizable templates

### LearnWorlds

- **Custom domain** on all paid plans
- Full CNAME setup with automatic SSL
- **Advanced site builder** with drag-and-drop page editor — one of the most flexible visual builders in this comparison
- Template library with customizable themes
- Support for root domains and subdomains

### Podia

- **Custom domain** on paid plans
- Automatic SSL
- Basic DNS setup
- The "Powered by Podia" badge can be removed on the **Shaker plan** ($89/month) and above
- Lower plans show Podia branding on the site

### Docebo

- **Custom domain** included in all plans
- Full white-label domain configuration
- **Multiple branded portals** through the Extended Enterprise feature — each portal gets its own domain, branding, and visual identity
- This is critical for companies serving multiple client audiences or business units

### Absorb LMS

- **Custom domain** included
- Full DNS configuration
- SSL provisioned automatically
- Portal-level domain customization for different audiences

### TalentLMS

- **Custom domain** supported on paid plans
- Subdomain on TalentLMS domain available on free/starter tiers (`yourbrand.talentlms.com`)
- Custom domain requires DNS CNAME setup
- SSL included

## Branded Mobile Apps

Mobile apps are where branding matters most to many learners — the app name, icon, and store listing directly affect perceived professionalism and brand recognition.

### Thinkific

Thinkific offers **Branded Mobile** — a no-code mobile app builder that creates a fully branded iOS and Android app published under your own Apple App Store and Google Play developer accounts.

Key features:
- **Your brand name** in the app store (not "Thinkific")
- **Custom app icon, splash screen, and color scheme**
- Push notifications to re-engage learners
- Offline content access
- In-app purchases supported
- Community features available in-app
- Available on **Grow plan** and above (or as a Thinkific Plus feature)

This is a genuine white-label app — learners see your brand, not Thinkific's. The app is managed through Thinkific's admin but published to your developer accounts.

### Kajabi

Kajabi offers a **Branded Mobile App** with similar capabilities:
- Custom app name, icon, and branding in app stores
- Published under your Apple and Google developer accounts
- Push notifications
- Branded login experience
- Full course and community access
- Customizable app layout and navigation
- Available on **Growth plan** and above

Kajabi has invested heavily in its branded app experience, with a dedicated customization interface for managing the app's look and feel without code.

### Teachable

Teachable provides a **branded iOS app** (and learner access via mobile web on Android). The Teachable mobile app can be customized with:
- School name and branding
- Custom colors and logo
- Course content delivery
- However, the app is **published under Teachable's app store listing**, not your own brand — learners download the "Teachable" app and then find your school within it

This is a meaningful distinction. Unlike Thinkific and Kajabi, Teachable's mobile experience is not fully white-labeled — your brand appears inside Teachable's app container.

### LearnWorlds

LearnWorlds offers a **branded mobile app** for iOS and Android:
- Custom app name, icon, and splash screen
- Published under your own developer accounts
- Push notifications
- Offline learning support
- In-app community access
- Available on **Learning Center plan** ($249/month) and above, or as an add-on

LearnWorlds' branded app is fully white-labeled and competitive with Thinkific and Kajabi in this regard.

### Podia

- **No branded mobile app** — Podia does not offer a native mobile app for learners
- Content is accessible via mobile-responsive web design
- This is a notable gap for experts who want a mobile-first brand experience

### Docebo

Docebo offers a **white-label mobile app** for iOS and Android:
- Fully branded with your organization's name, icon, and visual identity
- Published under your developer accounts
- Push notifications
- Offline content access
- Deep linking support
- The mobile app can be configured per **Extended Enterprise portal**, meaning different business units or client groups can have different app experiences
- Available on enterprise plans

Docebo's mobile app is enterprise-grade and designed for organizations with complex branding requirements across multiple audiences.

### Absorb LMS

Absorb offers both a **generic Absorb app** and a **white-label branded app**:
- Custom branding, app name, and icon
- Published to your developer accounts
- Push notifications
- Offline learning support
- Available as an add-on or on enterprise plans

### TalentLMS

TalentLMS provides a **generic mobile app** branded as TalentLMS:
- Learners download the TalentLMS app and log in to your portal
- Custom branding within the app (colors, logo) is possible
- But the app store listing shows **TalentLMS**, not your brand
- No fully white-labeled app option

## Removing Platform Branding

Beyond domains and apps, platform branding appears in footers, emails, login pages, and various UI elements. How much of it can you remove?

### Thinkific

- **"Powered by Thinkific" removal:** Available on Growth plan and above
- Full control over site footer content
- Custom email templates with your branding
- Login page customization
- Certificate branding customization
- Course player is clean and doesn't display Thinkific branding prominently
- On lower plans, subtle Thinkific branding may appear

### Kajabi

- **No "Powered by Kajabi" badge** on any paid plan — Kajabi is relatively clean about its own branding
- Full control over site footer, header, and navigation
- Custom email design with your branding
- Login pages are fully brandable
- Checkout pages display your brand only
- The platform is designed to keep Kajabi invisible to your customers

### Teachable

- **"Powered by Teachable" removal:** Available on Pro plan and above
- Custom branding on school pages
- Email customization with your logo and colors
- Course player has minimal Teachable branding
- On Basic plan, Teachable branding appears in the footer

### LearnWorlds

- **"Powered by LearnWorlds" removal:** Available on Pro Trainer plan and above
- Extensive site builder customization
- Custom email templates
- Branded certificates
- Login and registration page customization
- LearnWorlds is relatively aggressive about letting you customize every touchpoint

### Podia

- **"Powered by Podia" removal:** Available on Shaker plan ($89/month) and above
- Lower plans show Podia branding on your site
- Email customization available
- Basic footer and page customization

### Docebo

- **Full white-label** — Docebo branding is completely removable
- Admins can configure every visual element: login pages, headers, footers, email templates, certificates
- Extended Enterprise portals allow completely different branding per audience
- No "Powered by Docebo" on any plan (it's enterprise software — white-labeling is expected)

### Absorb LMS

- **Full white-label** on enterprise plans
- Complete branding customization: colors, logos, fonts, layouts
- Email templates fully customizable
- No Absorb branding visible to learners when white-labeled
- Portal-level branding for different audiences

### TalentLMS

- **"Powered by TalentLMS" removal:** Available on higher-tier plans
- Custom logo, colors, and themes on all paid plans
- Email customization with branding
- Basic login page customization
- Full white-label is limited compared to Docebo and Absorb

## Email & Communication Branding

Emails sent to learners (enrollment confirmations, progress reminders, certificates) are a branding touchpoint that's easy to overlook.

### Thinkific
- Custom email templates with your logo, colors, and branding
- Emails sent from your domain (with proper DKIM/SPF configuration)
- Automated email sequences with branded design
- TCommerce transaction emails are branded to your business

### Kajabi
- Full email marketing platform built in
- Complete design control over all emails
- Emails sent from your configured domain
- Broadcast emails, sequences, and automated emails all fully branded
- Kajabi's email system is one of the most customizable in this category

### Teachable
- Custom email templates
- School branding in automated emails
- Limited email marketing (basic announcements)
- Custom "from" address configuration

### LearnWorlds
- Custom email templates with visual editor
- Branded automated emails
- Custom sender address
- Email notification customization per event type

### Podia
- Built-in email marketing with customizable templates
- Branded transactional emails
- Custom sender address
- Newsletter and broadcast email tools

### Enterprise Platforms (Docebo, Absorb, TalentLMS)
All three support:
- Custom email templates
- Branded transactional emails
- Custom sender domains
- Notification customization per event type
- Docebo and Absorb additionally support email branding per portal/branch

## White-Label Reselling & Multi-Tenant Support

Some organizations need more than personal branding — they need to resell the LMS to their own clients, each with its own branding. This is true white-label reselling.

### Thinkific

Thinkific doesn't offer multi-tenant white-label reselling in its standard plans. **Thinkific Plus** (enterprise tier) supports:
- Multiple Thinkific sites managed under one account
- Bulk site management
- API-driven provisioning for partners

However, true white-label reselling (where your clients each get a branded LMS instance) is limited compared to purpose-built enterprise platforms.

### Kajabi

Kajabi does not natively support multi-tenant white-label reselling. Each Kajabi account is a single site. There's no partner/reseller program that lets you provision branded Kajabi instances for clients.

### Teachable

Similar to Kajabi — single-site accounts without multi-tenant or reseller capabilities. Teachable's API allows some programmatic management, but it's not designed for white-label reselling.

### LearnWorlds

LearnWorlds has been expanding its partner capabilities:
- **Network of Schools** feature for managing multiple school instances
- Potential for agency/partner models where you manage client schools
- Not a full white-label reselling system, but more flexible than Kajabi or Teachable

### Podia

No multi-tenant or white-label reselling capabilities.

### Docebo

This is where Docebo excels. The platform is built for multi-tenant scenarios:
- **Extended Enterprise** — create multiple branded portals from a single Docebo instance, each with unique domains, branding, content, and user management
- **Multi-tenant architecture** — isolate audiences while managing from one admin console
- **Partner/channel training** — sell access to branded training portals for external partners, franchises, or clients
- API-driven provisioning for automated portal creation

Docebo is the strongest option for organizations that need to provide branded learning experiences to multiple external audiences.

### Absorb LMS

Absorb supports multi-portal configurations:
- **Multiple branded portals** from a single instance
- Per-portal domain, branding, and content configuration
- Suitable for organizations with multiple business units or client-facing training programs
- Not as deep as Docebo's Extended Enterprise, but functional for multi-brand scenarios

### TalentLMS

TalentLMS supports **branches** — sub-portals within a single account that can have different branding:
- Each branch gets custom branding, user groups, and content assignments
- Useful for departments or client segmentation
- Branches share the same TalentLMS domain structure by default (custom domains per branch may require configuration)
- Less sophisticated than Docebo or Absorb for true white-label reselling

## Which Platform Fits Your Branding Needs?

### For Solo Experts Building a Personal Brand

**Thinkific, Kajabi, and LearnWorlds** offer the best combination of custom domains, branded mobile apps, and platform branding removal. All three let you present a fully branded web and mobile experience where your learners see only your brand.

Kajabi edges ahead on site design flexibility and built-in marketing customization. Thinkific and LearnWorlds are strong on mobile app branding. Choose based on which ecosystem fits your broader needs.

### For Academies Serving Multiple Programs

**LearnWorlds** and **Thinkific Plus** handle multi-program branding well. LearnWorlds' site builder is among the most flexible, letting you create visually distinct experiences for different programs. Thinkific Plus allows multiple sites under one account for academies running distinct brands.

### For Companies Training External Partners

**Docebo** is the clear leader for multi-tenant, white-label scenarios. If you need to deliver branded training portals to multiple clients, partners, or franchises — each with their own domain, app, and visual identity — Docebo's Extended Enterprise feature is purpose-built for this. **Absorb LMS** offers similar multi-portal capabilities at a slightly smaller scale.

### For Budget-Conscious Sellers

**Podia** provides basic branding at an affordable price point, but the lack of a branded mobile app and the requirement to pay for badge removal limit its white-label potential. **TalentLMS** offers custom domains and basic branding customization, adequate for internal training but not ideal for external-facing branded experiences.

---

## The Bottom Line

Branding capabilities should be a non-negotiable factor in your LMS decision. The gap between "custom domain with some color changes" and "fully white-labeled web and mobile experience" is significant — and it directly affects how your learners perceive your business.

For expert-led businesses, the branded mobile app is increasingly important as mobile learning grows. Thinkific, Kajabi, and LearnWorlds all deliver genuine white-label apps published under your own brand. Teachable's app-within-an-app approach and Podia's lack of any native app are meaningful limitations.

For enterprise and B2B scenarios, Docebo's multi-tenant architecture sets the standard. If you're evaluating white-label reselling or multi-audience branding, Docebo should be on your shortlist regardless of other considerations.
