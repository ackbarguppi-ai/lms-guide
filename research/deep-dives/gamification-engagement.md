## Key Findings
- TalentLMS: most comprehensive built-in gamification (points, badges, leaderboards, levels)
- Docebo: AI-powered gamification, contests, social learning
- Thinkific: community-driven engagement (Communities), certificates, progress tracking
- Kajabi: community features but minimal gamification
- LearnWorlds: badges and interactive elements
- Teachable: minimal engagement features
- Community is highest-impact engagement tool (3-5x completion rates)
- Average course completion: 5-15% without engagement features
